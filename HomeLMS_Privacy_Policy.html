Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Thank you for using Home LMS. This Privacy Policy explains how Home LMS Team handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy — your library data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Home LMS is designed with your privacy in mind. The App operates primarily on your device and does not require account registration.

Information stored locally on your device:

  • Book inventory data (titles, authors, ISBN, categories, notes, cover images)
  • App preferences (theme settings, display options)
  • API keys you choose to enter (stored securely using device encryption)

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following to our servers:

  • Your personal identity or contact information
  • Your book inventory or reading habits
  • Your location data
  • Your device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

All your library data stays entirely on your device at all times.

3. Third-Party Services

Home LMS optionally integrates with third-party APIs to power certain features. These are only activated when you enter your own API keys in the App settings.

Google APIs (Books API, Places API):

  • Used to fetch book details from ISBN scans and find nearby bookshops
  • Your API key is stored securely on your device
  • Governed by Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy

Google Gemini API:

  • Used to power the AI Librarian chat and "Tell me more about this book" feature
  • Your API key is stored securely on your device
  • Book titles and queries are sent to Google's servers to generate responses
  • Governed by Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy
⚠️ Important: You provide your own API keys. We do not provide or have access to your API keys. You are responsible for your own API usage and any associated costs.

4. Camera and Photo Library

The App requests access to your device camera and photo library solely to:

  • Scan ISBN barcodes to look up book details
  • Photograph book covers for identification and storage
  • Upload existing photos as book cover images

Camera and photo data is processed locally on your device or sent to Google's APIs for book identification only when you initiate a scan. We do not store, transmit, or share any camera or photo data.

5. Location Data

The App may request your approximate location solely to find nearby bookshops when you search for a book not in your library. Location data is:

  • Only accessed when you explicitly tap "Find Nearby Bookshops"
  • Sent to Google Places API to return relevant results
  • Never stored on our servers or shared with any third party beyond Google Places API

6. Data Storage and Security

All your library data is stored locally on your device using SQLite, a standard mobile database. Your API keys are stored using your device's secure encrypted storage. We do not have access to any data stored on your device.

You can delete all app data at any time through Settings > Clear All Data within the App, or by uninstalling the App.

7. Children's Privacy

Home LMS is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • View all your data directly within the App
  • Export your library to Excel at any time
  • Delete all data through Settings > Clear All Data
  • Uninstall the App to remove all associated local data
